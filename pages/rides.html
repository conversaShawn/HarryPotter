Flight of the Hippogriff™

Enter the Care of Magical Creatures grounds and pass by Hagrid's hut. As you make your way onto the ride, Hagrid gives instructions on the proper way to approach a Hippogriff. The Flight of the Hippogriff™ is a family-friendly roller coaster that spirals and dives around the pumpkin patch, and swoops past Hagrid's hut.
